type: case
source_chapter: Ch4 Genres
source_quote: "Michael Clayton may have stumped the marketing department at Warner Brothers... Michael Clayton is a type of movie I call 'Institutionalized.'"

id: c14
title: King Kong — Buddy Love 而非 MITH
type: case
source_chapter: Ch4 Genres
source_quote: "King Kong does not... what it's 'about' is the love story between Naomi Watts and Mr. Big and Tall."
summary: |
  King Kong 看起来是 Monster in the House,但实际上是 Buddy Love。
  MITH 要求:怪物+封闭空间+罪(某人做了错事放进怪物)。Kong 不满足。
  Kong 更像 Old Yeller、E.T.、Rain Man——关于爱一个特殊的、世界不理解的人的风险与回报。
  判断类型时要问"这个故事是'关于'什么的",而非表面元素。
tags: [genre, classification, buddy-love, case-study]
