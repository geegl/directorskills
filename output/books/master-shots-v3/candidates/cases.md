# Candidates — Cases
## Book: 大师镜头 第三卷 (Master Shots Vol. 3)

### C1: 《命运规划局》— 斜式运动对角线推进
**Technique**: 2.8 斜式运动
**Description**: Matt Damon runs through the city while camera tracks at a near-180° angle. The camera stays at the same distance, creating intense speed through perspective distortion.
**Lesson**: Near-perpendicular camera-to-actor angle maximizes speed perception.

### C2: 《银翼杀手》— 旋转出画/弧线运动
**Technique**: 4.7 旋转出画
**Description**: Rutger Hauer and Joe Turkel in a confrontation. Camera arcs around Hauer while he arcs around Turkel, creating opposing arcs that trap Turkel.
**Lesson**: Counter-arcing camera and actor movements create spatial entrapment metaphor.

### C3: 《这个杀手不太冷》— 俯瞰后拉建立威压
**Technique**: 5.4 俯瞰后拉
**Description**: Camera above Frank Senger, dollying backward while he strides forward. Despite high angle, his forward motion + not looking up = dominance.
**Lesson**: High angle + forward movement + no upward gaze = overwhelming presence.

### C4: 《盗梦空间》— 广角近拍紧迫感
**Technique**: 1.6 广角镜头近拍
**Description**: Wide lens near actors exaggerates their movement through space. Small camera movement + large actor movement = urgency.
**Lesson**: Wide lens amplifies the ratio of actor movement to camera movement.

### C5: 《辛德勒的名单》— 固定广角长镜头舞蹈
**Technique**: 1.7 固定广角镜头
**Description**: Spielberg uses a near-static wide-angle camera while two actors perform a "dance" of seven different compositions in a single take.
**Lesson**: Pre-plan 6-7 best compositions in the space, then choreograph actors to hit those marks naturally.

### C6: 《真爱至上》— 越轴剪辑
**Technique**: 4.1 越轴
**Description**: Camera crosses the 180° axis during a conversation scene, motivated by a sound. The actor's head-turn bridges the jump cut.
**Lesson**: Axis crossing is justified when motivated by a story event; bridge with actor movement.

### C7: 《艾利之书》— 权力交换通过摄影机接近
**Technique**: 5.5 权力交换
**Description**: Camera starts near Denzel Washington (we identify with him). As Gary Oldman approaches the camera, the dolly retreats but Oldman catches up. After the cut, camera stays near Oldman — he has "taken" the camera from Washington.
**Lesson**: Which actor the camera stays near after a cut determines who the audience feels is in control.

### C8: 《别让我走》— 穿过人群取景
**Technique**: 4.4 穿过人群
**Description**: Camera shoots through a group of students. Nathalie Richard is small and distant in frame while students are large and out-of-focus, establishing her vulnerability.
**Lesson**: Shooting through a crowd with protagonist far away = feeling of being overwhelmed.

### C9: 《人工智能》— 镜面反射多层次叙事
**Technique**: 3.6 镜面反射
**Description**: Spielberg uses windshield reflection to show separation between mother and child. Rear-view mirror creates a diminishing shot. Metal surface reflection creates distorted POV.
**Lesson**: Reflections create simultaneous multi-layer information without cutting.

### C10: 《斗士》— 复杂旋转+焦点引导
**Technique**: 10.1 复杂旋转
**Description**: Camera orbits Wahlberg while focus shifts between foreground and background actors, ending on Wahlberg's close-up. The arc + focus changes convey his internal decision-making.
**Lesson**: Combine arc movement with focus pulls to guide attention through a complex scene.

### C11: 《罪孽天使》— 双重推进象征吸引
**Technique**: 5.1 双重推进
**Description**: Two over-shoulder shots with simultaneous push-ins toward each actor. Despite being in separate frames, the camera movement shows their mutual attraction.
**Lesson**: Pushing toward both actors in alternating shots = visual metaphor for mutual attraction.

### C12: 《战马》— 道具引导摄影机运动
**Technique**: 11.2 激发摄影机
**Description**: Emily Watson pulls a carrot from the ground; camera follows the carrot upward, arriving naturally at her face. Actor's hand movement motivates camera direction change.
**Lesson**: Props and physical actions can motivate camera movement that would otherwise feel unmotivated.

### C13: 《荒岛余生》— 亲自揭示/演员交接
**Technique**: 6.7 亲自揭示
**Description**: A boy walks through the room, guiding the camera along an arc. Camera transfers from boy to Tom Hanks descending a ladder. Single shot establishes space, introduces protagonist, delivers story point.
**Lesson**: Design camera path first, then actor blocking to "hand off" camera focus between characters.

### C14: 《月桂谷》— 融合运动空间叙事
**Technique**: 2.1 融合运动
**Description**: Simple right-dolly combined with actors moving away from camera through bushes. The dolly + actor movement simultaneously establishes space, introduces characters, and conveys their discomfort.
**Lesson**: A single simple camera movement combined with actor blocking can replace multiple separate setups.

### C15: 《功夫梦》— 环绕运动+距离变化
**Technique**: 7.6 环绕运动
**Description**: Camera orbits a training scene. Distance variation (close then pull back) + passing behind a column create perceived speed increase.
**Lesson**: Vary distance during orbit and pass objects in foreground to accelerate perceived pace.
