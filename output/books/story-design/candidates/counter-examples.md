id: ce01
title: Evan Almighty — "comeuppance"前提杀死票房
type: counter-example
source_chapter: Ch1 Ideas and Concepts
source_quote: "It's fun to have the powers of God, but no fun to be under God's thumb cursed by having to build an ark."
summary: |
  失败模式:前提的"Fun and Games"不够有趣。
  Bruce Almighty 是 Empowerment(拥有神力),Evan 是 Comeuppance(被迫造方舟)。
  教训:在 pitch 阶段就要问"这个前提听起来有趣吗?观众会'跑着'去看吗?"
tags: [concept, failure, empowerment]

id: ce02
title: Spider-Man 3 — "spidering"导致故事散乱
type: counter-example
source_chapter: Ch1 Ideas and Concepts
source_quote: "We get insecure that our tale isn't enough sometimes and we tend to 'fix it' by running away from it and building up B, C, D, and E stories."
summary: |
  失败模式:不安全感导致用"更多故事"替代"讲好一个故事"。
  四种魔法+四个反派 = Quadruple Mumbo Jumbo。
  Blake 在工作坊中观察到同样的问题:创作者不信任自己的故事,用更多故事填充。
  术语:"spidering"——从主线故事偏离到支线。
tags: [failure, story-focus, double-mumbo-jumbo]

id: ce03
title: State of Play — 标题无法传达内容
type: counter-example
source_chapter: Ch3 Titles & Loglines
source_quote: "I still don't know what the title refers to, and that had a lot to do with its poor showing."
summary: |
  失败模式:标题不说"它是什么"。
  即使有 Russell Crowe,即使看完电影,仍不知道标题指什么。
  观众在购票窗口根据标题做决定——如果标题模糊,他们会跳过。
tags: [title, marketing, failure]

id: ce04
title: Body of Lies — 通用标题无信息量
type: counter-example
source_chapter: Ch3 Titles & Loglines
source_quote: "Its title, Body of Lies, is one of those generic ones, like the infamous For Love or Money. It told me nothing."
summary: |
  失败模式:通用标题 = 适用于任何电影的标题。
  For Love or Money 可以是《乱世佳人》也可以是《教父》。
  Body of Lies 不暗示任何特定类型、设定或冲突。
  好标题应该让观众在看到的一刻就知道"它是什么"。
tags: [title, marketing, failure, generic]

id: ce05
title: Dreamcatcher — Quadruple Mumbo Jumbo
type: counter-example
source_chapter: Ch6 Structure
source_quote: "This is due to its use of e.s.p., aliens, germ warfare, AND predicting the future. To me, it's too much to 'buy.'"
summary: |
  失败模式:一部电影叠加四种不同的"魔法"设定。
  Dreamcatcher 同时使用 ESP+外星人+细菌战+预知未来。
  即使是 Stephen King 写的也越过了"观众能接受"的界限。
  结果:非常混乱的电影。
tags: [failure, world-building, double-mumbo-jumbo]

id: ce06
title: On-the-Nose 对白 — 角色"谈论他们在做的事"
type: counter-example
source_chapter: Ch7 Dialogue
source_quote: "If you have scenes where characters are 'talking about what they're doing,' it's time for a triage."
summary: |
  失败模式:角色对白直接说出他们正在做的事。
  这是"on-the-nose"写作——没有潜台词,没有角色区分。
  诊断:盖住角色名字,看能否分辨谁在说话。如果不能,说明所有角色说话方式相同。
  常见原因:角色是作者的"代言人"而非独立个体。
tags: [dialogue, failure, character-voice]

id: ce07
title: "Redemption" 式标题 — 主题代替具体
type: counter-example
source_chapter: Ch3 Titles & Loglines
source_quote: "If your story is about redemption and so you call it… Redemption, take a breath. Try something new."
summary: |
  失败模式:直接用主题作为标题。
  如果故事关于救赎就叫"Redemption",关于爱就叫"For Love or Money"。
  好标题应该"说它是什么"但带一个转折——不能太直白,也不能太模糊。
  解法:找到标题的 irony,让标题本身就是一个 hook。
tags: [title, failure, concept]
