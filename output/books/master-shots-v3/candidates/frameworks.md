# Candidates — Frameworks
## Book: 大师镜头 第三卷 (Master Shots Vol. 3)

### F1: 镜头-情绪映射框架 (Lens-Emotion Mapping)
**Source**: Chapter 1 (1.1–1.10)
**Description**: A systematic framework mapping focal length choices to emotional effects. Long telephoto isolates and creates intimacy; wide-angle exaggerates space and creates urgency; medium focal length is neutral and observational. The framework includes movement-lens combinations: telephoto+dolly = amplified isolation; wide-angle+tracking = exaggerated speed.
**Key Quote**: "长焦镜头能够让背景虚化变得更容易，让演员与画面相分离。这种效果非常适合于戏剧化瞬间或揭示场面" (1.4)
**Key Quote**: "广角镜头夸大了距离，因此摄影机运动显得更迅速" (1.6)
**Cross-ref**: Related to speed conveyance (7.7)

### F2: 象征性场景调度框架 (Symbolic Staging Framework)
**Source**: Chapter 5 (5.1–5.8)
**Description**: A framework for encoding narrative meaning through camera position and movement rather than dialogue. Includes: double push for attraction, power exchange via camera proximity, intimacy interruption via depth of field, breaking group scenes through actor repositioning.
**Key Quote**: "每个镜头都应该揭示演员、传达故事，但技术运用最熟练的导演会力图在机位设置中传达象征性意义" (5.1)
**Cross-ref**: Related to power exchange (5.5), actor-driven camera (2.3)

### F3: 权力-距离框架 (Power-Distance Framework)
**Source**: 5.5, 5.4, 9.10
**Description**: Whoever is closer to the camera holds perceived power. When one actor approaches the camera while the dolly retreats, the approaching actor "takes over" the scene. Combines with camera height (low angle = power, high angle = vulnerability unless actor doesn't look up).
**Key Quote**: "谁离摄影机更近，我们就会认为谁控制着状况" (5.5)
**Key Quote**: "摄影机放置的位置对于表现场景里谁掌控着权力平衡有着巨大的影响" (5.5)

### F4: 演员驱动摄影机框架 (Actor-Motivated Camera Framework)
**Source**: 2.3, 6.7, 11.2
**Description**: Camera movement should always be motivated by actor action or story events, not directorial preference. Actors physically guide the camera through space. In "亲自揭示" (6.7), one actor leads the camera through a scene, then "hands off" to a second actor.
**Key Quote**: "运动对摄影机进行引导...如果你希望观众沉浸在故事之中，一定要让摄影机运动是由画面中的动作所驱动的" (11.2)
**Key Quote**: "摄影机是为故事服务的，而不是去做其他事情" (3.7)

### F5: 速度传达框架 (Speed Conveyance Framework)
**Source**: 7.7, 2.2, 7.1, 7.6
**Description**: A multi-layered framework for conveying speed without fast cutting. Techniques include: near-180° camera angle matching actor path, low angle for ground proximity, wide-angle lens for peripheral distortion, foreground objects creating motion blur, distance variation during orbit.
**Key Quote**: "以几乎相对的角度进行拍摄，一个短镜头能比一堆快速剪辑创造出更多的速度感" (7.7)
**Key Quote**: "让摄影机和演员之间出现物体。当这些物体闪过，形成虚化，效果便能得到强化" (2.2)

### F6: 纵深填充框架 (Depth-Filling Framework)
**Source**: 11.1, 6.3, 3.1, 3.2
**Description**: Using foreground, midground, and background layers to create visual density and depth. Background actors moving through frame create energy without distraction. Foreground actors (even out of focus) create crowd/threat effect. Camera direction changes can be motivated by background movement.
**Key Quote**: "处于焦点之外的背景移动是完美的选择" (11.1)
**Key Quote**: "让背景的演员成为画面主体，可创造出一种房间拥挤热闹的感觉" (6.3)
