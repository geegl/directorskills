# Candidates — Principles
## Book: 大师镜头 第三卷 (Master Shots Vol. 3)

### P1: 肢体表演优先原则 (Body Performance Over Face)
**Source**: 8.2, 2.7
**Description**: When staging scenes of emotional tension between characters, body language and posture are as important as facial expressions. Use wide/medium shots to capture physical acting. Actors should be told you're shooting this way so they adjust performance accordingly.
**Key Quote**: "一般的导演可能倾向于使用特写，但身体动作在演员内心挣扎着是否要交流和接触的场景里更加重要" (8.2)
**Key Quote**: "有些演员希望他们的脸能一直出现在屏幕上。如果你的演员尝试那样做，很可能会毁掉这一类场景" (2.7)

### P2: 停止跟随即留恋原则 (Camera Stop = Lingering Emotion)
**Source**: 8.9
**Description**: When an actor walks away from another actor, letting the camera stop following them (rather than tracking along) signals that the emotional moment lingers. The viewer feels the separation.
**Key Quote**: "让摄影机留在原地，让他走开，就会感觉到这一刻挥之不去，难以忘怀" (8.9)

### P3: 焦点即认同原则 (Focus = Identification)
**Source**: 5.5, 3.2, 10.2
**Description**: Whoever is in focus, the audience identifies with. Shifting focus between actors is a tool for shifting audience sympathy. In multi-actor scenes, focus controls who the audience is "with."
**Key Quote**: "摄影机接近演员的位置（无论我们是从哪儿看他的）帮助我们对他产生认同" (5.5)

### P4: 动机驱动移动原则 (Motivated Movement Only)
**Source**: 1.7, 2.7, 3.7, 6.3
**Description**: Every actor movement must be motivated by story (going somewhere, looking at something, talking to someone). Never move actors purely for visual effect. Unmotivated movement breaks credibility.
**Key Quote**: "每一个远离或接近摄影机的运动，如让演员去什么地方或者看什么东西，或者对另外一个人说话，都应该由某一个动机驱使" (1.7)
**Key Quote**: "不要仅仅为了配合镜头让演员移动，务必让演员的移动是有意义的" (3.7)

### P5: 运动对比原则 (Movement Contrast Principle)
**Source**: 11.3, 7.3, 2.9
**Description**: Effective camera work requires contrast — fast against slow, moving against still, close against distant. A scene with constant camera movement at constant speed becomes monotonous. Vary pace within and between shots.
**Key Quote**: "处于持续运动的摄影机会让人觉得没有目的地四处游移，尤其在摄影机步调不发生改变的时候" (11.3)
**Key Quote**: "两种不同步调的运动的对比会让这个镜头变得有趣" (7.3)

### P6: 构图先于摄影机原则 (Blocking Before Camera)
**Source**: 8.3, 4.8, 10.4
**Description**: When designing close-ups and dialogue scenes, decide actor positioning before camera placement. The actors' angles relative to each other and the camera create the emotional texture.
**Key Quote**: "拍摄特写或者对话时，你在考虑怎么放置摄影机之前可能就要先考虑演员的位置" (8.3)

### P7: 意外揭示原则 (Unexpected Reveal Principle)
**Source**: 3.4, 3.5, 4.6
**Description**: Break audience expectations by having actors enter their own POV shots, or by revealing unexpected subjects through camera movement. Creates unease, surprise, or mystery.
**Key Quote**: "让人意外的是，穆里根随后从左侧入画，走到了场景中。实质上，演员走进了她自己的视点里" (3.4)

### P8: 剪影悬念原则 (Silhouette as Suspense)
**Source**: 4.8
**Description**: Using silhouette (background brighter than subject) forces audience to speculate about the actor's thoughts and intentions. The actor must adjust physical performance since facial expression is invisible.
**Key Quote**: "剪影首先是让观众猜测演员到底在想什么。这一点是很讽刺的，因为演员无法通过面部表情而只能通过身体语言来表达这些想法" (4.8)

### P9: 简单运动复合效果原则 (Simple Movement, Complex Effect)
**Source**: 2.1, 3.1, 5.8
**Description**: The most powerful shots often use the simplest camera movements combined with carefully planned actor blocking. A basic lateral dolly with well-choreographed actors can replace ten separate setups.
**Key Quote**: "设计镜头时，找出要从剧本中挖掘的故事元素，看看结合了演员在场景中移动的简单摄影机运动是否能给你想要的一切。如果一个镜头就能有效地运作，就没有必要设置十个不同的镜头" (2.1)

### P10: 方向变换需要动机原则 (Direction Change Needs Motivation)
**Source**: 11.1
**Description**: When the camera changes direction mid-shot (left to right, then forward), the change should be motivated by a background element (a vehicle, a passing actor) moving in the new direction.
**Key Quote**: "通常中景的方向变换是不协调的，不过很快背景中一辆卡车就从画面左侧开向右侧。这有助于弱化摄影机向右侧的运动" (11.1)
