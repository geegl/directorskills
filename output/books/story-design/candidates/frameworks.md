id: f03
title: The Board 40卡场景系统
type: framework
source_chapter: Ch6 Structure
source_quote: "we take the logline and 15 beats of your movie idea, and we put it up on the Board... and proceed to beat on it until we get the 40 scenes you need to start writing."
summary: |
  将故事拆解为40张索引卡,分4行排列: Act 1 (约10卡), Act 2a (约10卡),
  Act 2b (约10卡), Act 3 (约10卡)。每张卡用一句简单陈述描述场景。
  The Board 是从15节拍到实际写作的桥梁——"如果你能 pitch 全部40卡,你就准备好写了"。
tags: [structure, scene-planning, tool]

type: framework
source_chapter: Ch3 Titles & Loglines
source_quote: "The basic components that must be included in a successful logline are: A type of protagonist, A type of antagonist, A conflict, An open-ended question."

  成功 logline 的4个基本要素:①有形容词的主角类型 ②有形容词的对手类型 ③火花四溅的冲突 ④开放式问题。
  额外4个加分要素:⑤irony(真正的hook)⑥令人兴奋的心理画面 ⑦观众和成本暗示 ⑧杀手级标题。
  好 logline 让人拍额头说"Doh!为什么我没想到!"

id: f05
title: Five-Step Finale (五步终局法)
type: framework
source_chapter: Ch6 Structure
source_quote: "Think of every Finale in terms of 'storming the castle.' Step 1: plan. Step 2: execute. Step 3: trap. Step 4: dig deep down. Step 5: new plan wins."
summary: |
  Act Three 的5步结构:①英雄和团队制定"攻城"计划 ②计划开始执行,一切顺利 ③到达目标发现是陷阱——公主不在塔里!
  ④英雄必须"深挖"找到最后的力量(即对不可见力量的信念) ⑤临场应变,发现最好的自己,执行新计划获胜。
  此序列可见于 Gladiator, Die Hard, Star Wars, Enchanted。
tags: [structure, finale, act-three, framework]

id: f06
title: Synthesis (正题-反题-合题) 三世界模型
type: framework
source_chapter: Ch6 Structure
source_quote: "You may call it Act One – Act Two – Act Three. I call it Thesis – Anti-thesis – Synthesis."
summary: |
  三幕结构的哲学基础: Act 1 = Thesis (英雄的正常世界), Act 2 = Anti-thesis (正常世界的镜像翻转),
  Act 3 = Synthesis (1+2=3,英雄整合旧我与所学,找到第三条路)。
  Synthesis 时刻是故事满足感的来源——Rose Dawson, Dudley 做爱时放 Bolero。
tags: [structure, three-acts, philosophy]

title: Story Spine 三点定位法
type: framework
source_chapter: Ch6 Structure
source_quote: "Step One of any story breaking adventure must begin with only three of those points: The Opening Image, The Final Image, and The Midpoint."
summary: |
  故事拆解的第一步只需三个点:①Opening Image(开场快照)②Final Image(终场快照)③Midpoint(神经中枢)。
  开场和终场必须是"相反的",Midpoint 是故事的"crack it"点——包含 false victory/defeat、stakes raised、boy-girl kiss。
