# Candidates — Counter-Examples
## Book: 大师镜头 第三卷 (Master Shots Vol. 3)

### CE1: 推进过快产生喜剧效果 (Push-In Too Fast = Comedy)
**Source**: 2.6
**Description**: When pushing the camera toward an actor from a great distance, if you push too fast and lose the motivating actor movement, the result risks comic effect rather than dramatic.
**Key Quote**: "如果我们推得太快，失去了这个演员的移动，就会有意外的喜剧性风险"

### CE2: 长焦镜头+斯坦尼康 = 灾难 (Telephoto + Steadicam = Disaster)
**Source**: 1.4
**Description**: Telephoto lenses exaggerate every camera shake. Using a telephoto with handheld or Steadicam in a scene requiring movement is inadvisable.
**Key Quote**: "即使只是轻微的摇晃，摄影机所有的侧向运动都会导致夸张的效果。这意味着在可能有大量运动镜头诸如斯坦尼康镜头或手持摄影的场景中，都不宜使用长焦镜头"

### CE3: 无动机移动 = 假 (Unmotivated Movement = Fake)
**Source**: 1.7, 6.3
**Description**: Having actors walk around a room without story motivation to create visual interest. The audience senses artifice.
**Key Quote**: "不要仅仅为了获取华丽的视觉效果而让他们四处转悠" (2.7)
**Key Quote**: "不要把你的演员看作移动道具" (4.4)

### CE4: 跟拍长镜头过度使用 (Overuse of Tracking Long Takes)
**Source**: 10.5
**Description**: Directors sometimes use elaborate tracking shots to show off technique. When the movement doesn't serve the story, it becomes monotonous and draws attention to the camera.
**Key Quote**: "你不能在整部电影里都以这样的方式拍摄，不然影片就会变得平庸"

### CE5: 持续游移手持摄影 (Constant Wandering Handheld)
**Source**: 11.3
**Description**: Handheld camera that never stops moving becomes boring, like a dull documentary. Without pace variation, even energetic scenes become tedious.
**Key Quote**: "处于持续运动的摄影机会让人觉得没有目的地四处游移"

### CE6: 广角镜头面部扭曲 (Wide-Angle Facial Distortion)
**Source**: 1.6
**Description**: Placing a wide-angle lens too close to a face creates unflattering distortion (enlarged nose). Mitigate by shooting from slightly above.
**Key Quote**: "离摄影机太近的事物都会显得比实际距离更近；这实际上意味着被摄主体看起来会有一个比正常尺寸还要略大的鼻子"

### CE7: 越轴无动机 (Unmotivated Axis Crossing)
**Source**: 4.1
**Description**: Crossing the 180° axis without a story event (sound, character movement) to justify it confuses the audience.
**Key Quote**: "如果将这个镜头偶然地出现在场景中央会将观众的注意力吸引到镜头上，让人感觉唐突，但因为它是我们听到声音的同时出现的...因此不会有问题"

### CE8: 大特写用于对话场景 (Extreme Close-Up for Dialogue)
**Source**: 8.4
**Description**: Extreme close-ups are effective for tension and reaction, but poor for dialogue scenes where the audience needs to see the full face and expression.
**Key Quote**: "当场景极度紧张、让人注意力高度集中或者人们等待着什么事情发生时，大特写才应被运用"

### CE9: 剪影过度使用 (Overuse of Silhouette)
**Source**: 4.8
**Description**: Too much silhouette frustrates the audience — hiding too much is as problematic as showing too much.
**Key Quote**: "不应该滥用剪影，因为隐藏太多事物会让观众产生挫败感"
